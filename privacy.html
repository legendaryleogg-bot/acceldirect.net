Privacy Policy

Effective Date: March 27, 2026

Who We Are

Accel Direct LLC ("we," "us," "our") is a business services company that connects caregivers with home care agencies through automated lead generation. We are located at 502 W 7th St, Ste 100, Erie, PA 16502.

Information We Collect

When you submit an inquiry through our intake forms, we may collect:

  • Name, email address, and phone number
  • Employment-related information you provide (county, insurance type, authorized hours)

How We Use Your Information

We use the information you provide to:

  • Evaluate your eligibility for caregiver placement opportunities
  • Present personalized offer information (pay rate, sign-on bonus)
  • Contact you by SMS and email regarding opportunities that match your profile
  • Connect you with partner agencies for potential employment

SMS Communications

By submitting your phone number through our intake form, you consent to receive text messages from Accel Direct LLC related to caregiver placement opportunities. Message and data rates may apply. Message frequency varies.

You may opt out at any time by replying STOP to any message. Reply HELP for assistance. Upon opting out, you will receive a single confirmation message and no further texts will be sent.

Information Sharing

We share your information only with the specific partner agency relevant to your placement opportunity, and only for the purpose of evaluating and onboarding you as a caregiver. We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information to third parties.

Data Retention

We retain your information for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes described above, or as required by applicable law. You may request deletion of your data at any time by contacting us.

Security

We use commercially reasonable measures to protect your information. However, no method of electronic transmission or storage is completely secure.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated effective date.
